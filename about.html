[ profile ]

I am a London-based artist, researcher, educator and creative technologist currently teaching games design and architecture at Barnet Southgate College.

In my practice, I explore the intersections between art, science, technology and human experience. My interests particularly focus on critical digital media studies and cyberfeminism. My research mainly explores glitch, network culture, authorship, and the strange emotional space created by contemporary digital systems.

[ research ]

My current enquiry is Glitch as Method: — Women-centered speculative design for rethinking identity and difference in cyber environments.

Questioning how glitch and technological failure can be employed as speculative design methods to reimagine representations of gender, otherness, and neurodiversity within digital and cyber spaces.

I am currently developing a practice-based research project exploring this.
