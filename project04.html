project 04: Reaction-diffusion: Interactive projection

[ Enquiry ]

To create an interactive installation inspired by reaction-diffusion, a mathematical model that corresponds to several physical phenomena. I was captivated by the way a computational system is capable of simulating the world around us to help us study the complexity of the natural world. My goal was to visualize the invisible aspects of our environment and encourage the audience to reflect on their own roles within this simulated ecosystem. This work was part of a group exhibition themed "The Arcade," featuring interactive installations, video art, music, and game design, emphasizing a playful and engaging atmosphere. As the group curator, I was pleased to oversee the project, ensuring a coherent experience that challenged the conventional, minimalist white-cube gallery space.

[ Medium and Software ]

TouchDesigner, wood and projection work, LEDs, and video.
